AYUSHMAAN CLASSES






1.) Two years Integrated classroom program:

Duration: Two Years during XI and XII
No of weeks: Approx 45
Course commencement: April/August
Eligibility: Students who have appeared for class X board exams.

Batch 1- Starting April
This is an innovative program which is meant for the student of class XI who wish to start preparing for the entrance examinations early. The program concentrates on clearing the basic concepts and then processed to the level of IIT-JEE. This is the best program which will help the students in both IIT-JEE and Board exams.Frequency of lectures in a week is 3-4 lectures per subject.
Batch 2-Starting July
This program is meant for those students who miss the opportunity to enroll themselves in Batch 1 Program in April. It also gives ample time to the student to make up his/her mindset for IIT-JEE/AIEEE. The duration of this program is 7 weeks less in comparison to Batch 1 program; however the Curriculum, Methodology & Study Material is absolutely a replica of Batch 1 program. This program is more intense and the frequency of lectures in a week in this program varies from 4-5 lectures per subject.

2.) One year Integrated classroom program:

Duration: One Year during class XII
No. of Hours: Approx 500 hours
Course commencement: April
Eligibility: Students who have appeared for class XI annual exams.

Batch- Starting April
The frequency of classes in this program is 3-4 lectures per subject per week. Regular periodic tests will be conducted to evaluate the learning of students. Students will be given Daily Practice Questions (DPQ) to work at their homes.

3.) Extended one year classroom program:

Duration: One Year after class XII
No. Of Weeks: Approx 36
Course commencement: June
Eligibility: Students who have cleared class XII Board exams.

Batch- Starting June
It will be assumed in this program that the students who have enrolled are thorough with the theoretical aspects of the subjects but lack the application part of the Formulae, Concepts & Approach of solving numerical problems. The concept of this course is to give a rigorous practice to the students for next 36 weeks of learning for IIT-JEE.

4.) Crash Course:

Duration:2 months
Course commencement: February
Eligibility: XII Studying or XII Pass.

Batch- Starting Feb.
This course is designed to provide extremely intense yet comprehensive training for the IIT- JEE, NIIT and other Entrance exams. The students who have managed till now without or with a little help for the preparation of IIT- JEE will get the crucial last push through our specially designed Crash program.
The program includes specifically & strategically formulated assignments with problem solving techniques besides intensive lectures and tests.

5.) Mock Test Series:

Duration:1 month
Course commencement: March
Eligibility: Anyone who has finished competitive course.

Batch- Starting March
The program includes specifically & strategically formulated assignments with problem solving techniques besides lectures and tests. Students are given various types of test based on the upcoming exams in which same paper pattern and marking scheme is there.

6.) Intense Training rank promoting program:

Duration:6 months
Course commencement: October
Eligibility: XII Studying or XII Pass.

Batch- Starting October
This is a medium duration program which includes specifically & strategically formulated assignments with problem solving techniques besides lectures and tests. Regular periodic tests along with Daily Practice Questions (DPQ) will be conducted to evaluate the learning of student.
The stimulating atmosphere of the institute, small batch size and one to one interaction with the faculty members will ensure that every rough edge is smoothened, every small doubt is cleared.
