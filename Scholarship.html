AYUSHMAAN CLASSES





At Ayushmaan Classes we have an elaborate program of scholarships for the meritorious students. We provide scholarship in the form of concession in Total Fee (excluding Admission fee) of the course to eligible students. The details of the various scholarships we offer are given below:



Category A: For the board toppers

A1. For the meritorious students of various boards:
60% Concession for Top 15 Merit Holders of Class X / XII in current session.

A2. For the meritorious students of CBSE/ICSE boards:
30% Concession for students who obtained 95% or above in Class X / XII in current session.

A3. For IIT JEE entrance test qualified students who wish to improve their rank.
There are two types of scholarships for such students:

(a) 75% concession for IIT-JEE 2012 qualified students (up to a rank of 9000,who wish to improve their rank secured in IIT-JEE)
(b) 50% concession for the students selected in AIEEE (up to a rank of 50000,who wish to improve their rank)

How to Apply for this Scholarship:

1. To avail the scholarship, student has to produce documents to prove the eligibility for the category A, within one month of course commencement date of X / XII class result declaration (whichever is later) at our Accounts Department.
2. Students for whom the X or XII board result is awaited at the time of admission can also apply for scholarship at later stage with all the above documents, if eligible.

Category B:

For the wards of Teachers, Engineers, Doctors, Defense Personnels, Single Parent and Siblings . The students who are sons/daughters of Teachers, Engineers, Doctors, Defense Personnels, Single Parent or who have a sibling studying in the institute will be given a scholarship of 10% on the Tuition Fee.

Category C:

For the students from SC/ST/OBC categories. The meritorious students from the SC/ST/OBC Categories will be given a scholarship of 20% on the tuition fee.

Category D:

Special Scholarships for the Poor Meritorious students. To fulfill our Social responsibility we will provide scholarships to the meritorious students from the lower strata of the society. We will teach them for free. Such students will have to furnish an income certificate and Original & a copy of the mark sheets of their board/School exams (The original mark sheet will be returned after the confirmation).

Conditions for offer of scholarship:

1. The amount or the percentage of the scholarship will include the tuition fee only and shall not include the Registration fee and the cost of the course material etc.
2. The Scholarships will be offered only after thorough examination of the relevant documents and the decision of the management committee well be final and binding in this regard.
3. A student can avail any one of the scholarship, whichever is the highest.
4. A student has to deposit the Total Course Fee (1st Installment) to become eligible for any scholarship.
5. Scholarship will be awarded through Account Payee Cheque only in the name of student.
6. The management committee of the institute may choose to give a scholarship on any other criteria to a deserving candidate apart from the above mentioned criteria. The decision of the management committee will be final and binding in this regard.
7. The management committee may chose to increase or decrease the amount or percentage of the scholarship at any time and the decision of the management committee will be final and binding in this regard.
8. In case a student chooses to leave the institute during the time of the course he/she will have to refund the full amount of the scholarship received by him/her.

Scholarship is likely to be withdrawn in case of:

I. If the student does not perform up to the mark in the class assessments, class tests, module tests, etc. In such a case a prior warning will be issued to the student and their parents.
II. If there is constant absenteeism in class lectures, class test, module test, etc.
III. If the student shows constant indisciplinary attitude with regards to the class mates, faculties, administrative staff and any other employee of the institute.
