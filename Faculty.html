HOW IS OUR TEACHING MODEL DIFFERENT?

Since we are not a coaching class, we do not focus on herding students. Instead we approach schools run by educationists with a vision and explore possibilities of forging alliances that not just take the student to the doors of the IITs and PMTs but also raises the standard and quality of education delivered during the crucial preparatory years.They are not merely the book worms instead they gain huge knowlegde which will help a lot in their bright future.

We take our ideas directly to students, their parents and teachers. We believe it is important that Ayushmaan communicates with its audience comprising students, their parents and audiences with integrity and least amount of false and fake promises due to which we communicate directly with our target audience and focus on generating fruitful long-term bonds.
